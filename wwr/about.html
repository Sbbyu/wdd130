History

Here at Riverbond Rafting we have a history of helping our clients forge strong familial bonds. The thrilling fun and team work required on White water rapids are a strong binding force that we have seen draw families closer together. Rafting has been a part of our family here at riverbond for 25 years. We would love to share the experiences our family has on the rapid to help your family draw closer together.

Our family has decades of experience in navigating the rivers on rafts, and we are here to bring the thrilling experience to you and your family in a safe manner so you can spend your adventure with peace of mind. We hope you grow closer to your loved ones just as we have grown closer to ours this past 25 years. Join us in building unbreakable bonds!
